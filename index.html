Tea From Assam

"Tea from Assam" takes readers on an engaging journey through the scenic tea gardens of Assam, offering insights into the history and importance of tea cultivation. The story follows two friends, Rajvir and Pranjol, as they travel to Assam during their vacation. Rajvir, fascinated by the beauty of the tea plantations and the history of tea, shares interesting facts about its origins in China and its introduction to India. The chapter beautifully blends the personal experience of the characters with the rich cultural and economic significance of tea in Assam, making it a captivating read for tea enthusiasts and history buffs alike.
